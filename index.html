One Trip

The Goals


During this unprecedented time of COVID-19 pandemic, we realize the struggle of having to wait long line-ups to get our essentials. We created OneTrip to help people finish their grocery shopping in one quick go, reducing the time spent outdoors during this season.

A Brief Summary of Features

  • Nearby Stores: Display a map with local markets / grocery stores
  • Wait Times: Show the latest estimate of wait time for each store
  • User-update: Users can update the wait times of stores on the go
